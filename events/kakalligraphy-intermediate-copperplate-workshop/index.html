This is the second half of a 2 part Copperplate Workshop held Sunday, August 30th at Wayfare in Costa Mesa from 2:30pm - 4:30pm. This fee includes the “Intermediate” learning experience, supplies as well as refreshments.

In this portion, students will enhance their knowledge more greatly while diving deep into the correct flourishing techniques, learning variations of the uppercase letters, and gaining a better overall comprehension of spacing.

You will go home with the following:

Knowledge of offhand flourishing techniques, and other options for uppercase letters.

Supplies include the following:

Straight Pen Holder Various Nibs Sumi Ink & Colored Ink Practice Sheets Exemplars of Copperplate lowercase, uppercase, flourishing and uppercase variations

Basic Calligraphy Knowledge Outline

**Supplies will be provided along with refreshments and sustainable snacks.

Purchase is required to save your space. Space is limited.
