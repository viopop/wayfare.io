The Space

We're Wayfare

... and we think you're pretty amazing. We are a 6,000 sq/ft coworking community, event space, and startup incubator in Costa Mesa, California seated between the John Wayne Airport and the thriving SOBECA district, outfitted with all of the coffee and awesome necessary for you to fix the broken world.

The space boasts over 3,000 square feet of highly ergonomic, productivity-inducing task seating, lounge areas ideal for a conversation and a break from the grind, semi-private alcoves, and two private meeting rooms for client meetings, strategy sessions, and staff meetings.

 

Early-stage or small organizations find their home in six private offices equipped with desks and chairs and exterior windows for natural light.

Wayfare is also ideal for workshops, conferences, gatherings, or parties as well as small client meetings or presentations. Join us at one of our upcoming events, or contact us if you'd like more information about hosting your event at Wayfare.
