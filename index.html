Let’s pursue the world’s best future together.

Wayfare is a 6,000 sq/ft industrial chic warehouse located in Costa Mesa, California seated between the John Wayne Airport and the thriving SOBECA district … with all the coffee and awesome to fuel you while you advance good in the world.

At Wayfare, we believe that everyone has something good to offer the world. Wayfare exists to provide people with the resources they need to put their good ideas into action and to keep at it even when it’s difficult. We do this through coworking, hosting events, workshops, and fundraisers, and through our start-up incubator program called Wayfare Labs.

So, whether you are a photographer who needs an occasional place to edit photos, a freelancer or small company who needs a full time workspace, or a you have a great idea that can change the world, we’d love for you to join us at Wayfare.
