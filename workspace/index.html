Collaborative Workspace

If your office has lately been an overcrowded coffee shop with bad Wi-Fi, then we know that you’re ready for a change.

Wayfare is more than a place to do some work (although it’s a great place for that). We are a coworking community of people who work, celebrate, create, and learn together. Wayfare is a catalyst to help launch ideas into action to impact the world for good.

We want you to be in community, but more importantly we want you to be in community with like minded people. As your biggest fans we are here to provide you with a communal and creative space so that you can continue doing what you do best.

Also, the free coffee, chandeliers, ergonomic chairs, custom made desks, and insanely comfortable lounge furniture are all pretty awesome too.

To schedule a tour, contact us and we’ll be happy to show you around. We’re open Monday-Friday 9-5 with extended hours available to members.
