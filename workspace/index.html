Collaborative Workspace

If your office has lately been an overcrowded coffee shop with bad Wi-Fi, then we know that you’re ready for a change.

Wayfare is more than a place to do some work or have client meetings (although it’s a great place for that). We are a coworking community of people who work, celebrate, create, and learn together.

Great people, free coffee, chandeliers, ergonomic chairs, custom made desks, and insanely comfortable lounge furniture all make for an inspiring and productive workplace.

To schedule a tour, contact us and we’ll be happy to show you around. We’re open Monday-Friday 9-5 with extended hours available to members. To book a meeting room see options below and fill out this form for a custom quote.

When you’re ready to sign up, just fill out the inquiry form and we’ll get back to you as quickly as possible.
